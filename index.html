How It Works

Three simple steps to perfect sparkling water

1. Fill

Add cold water to the bottle up to the fill line. Colder water holds carbonation better.

2. Fizz

Lock the bottle in place and press the button 1-3 times. Listen for the bubbles!

3. Flavor

Optional: add fresh lemon, herbs, or a drop of natural syrup for variety.

Is it safe to use? +
Yes, completely safe. We use food-grade CO₂ and a pressure-relief system that meets Vietnamese safety standards. All bottles are BPA-free.
How do CO₂ refills work? +
Simply swap your empty cylinder for a full one via our courier service. Order through our app or call, and we'll handle the rest. Fast, simple, and eco-friendly.
What about cleaning and maintenance? +
Very easy! Hand-wash the bottle with warm soapy water. Wipe the machine with a damp cloth. No electricity means no complex maintenance.

Statistics for Opportunity +
The Beverage Industry in Vietnam contributes significantly to the state budget with annual revenue reaching up to 60 trillion VND, accounting for 3.2% of total revenue. In terms of growth rate, according to the Vietnam Beer - Alcohol - Beverage Association, the beverage industry will maintain a growth rate of 7% per year.
Market Preferences +
Soft drinks and allied segments dominate the beverage landscape, comprising most of the production and consumption. The rapid uptrend in consumer demand, can be leveraged in shifting market preferences. With an increasing focus on nutrition and wellness, especially post–COVID-19, Vietnamese consumers are gravitating toward natural beverage options. SparkleViet’s health-forward positioning on no sugar resonates perfectly with this emerging consumer attitude.
Production in Vietnam +
Modernizing production through automation and eco-friendly aesthetic packaging has driven industry momentum. SparkleViet’s emphasis on refillable CO₂, BPA-free design, and plastic reduction aligns with these industry-wide trends toward sustainability.

Competition

Aquafina

Aquafina, a product of the Suntory PepsiCo VietNam corporation, officially entered the Vietnamese market in April 2002. The company's flagship product is purified water, which is produced through Pepsi's proprietary Hydro 7 filtration process, ensuring pure quality and excellent taste.

LaVie

Lavie, a renowned mineral water brand, is a member of Nestlé - the world's leading food and beverage company. Lavie joined Nestlé Water in 1992 and for the past 30 years, Lavie has continuously provided the highest quality products and services to its customers. Lavie's main products include large and small bottled natural mineral water. Lavie has a wide distribution network across Vietnam, including the Southern and Northern regions. Lavie had a not-so-smooth year in 2021 when both net revenue and net profit declined.
