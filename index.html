Country Overview & CAGE

Vietnam’s fast-growing, urban, and health-conscious market is primed for at-home beverage innovation.

Snapshot
  • ~98M population; median age ~30; rising middle class
  • HCMC: ~9M residents; Vietnam’s commercial hub
  • Household appliances market: strong growth
CAGE Highlights
  • Cultural: Café culture; wellness rising; bilingual site helps
  • Administrative: 100% foreign-owned LLC feasible
  • Geographic: Local manufacturing beats long lead times
  • Economic: Price-sensitive but premium-curious urban core

Good to Know: Legal, Taxes, Policy

Entity & Ownership
  • Wholly foreign-owned LLC (WFOE) common
  • Investment & Enterprise Registration needed
  • Retail of own product permitted; trading license if broader
Manufacturing & Compliance
  • Industrial-zone factory; fire & EHS approvals
  • Electrical safety (CR mark), food-grade CO₂
  • Labeling; cylinder transport safety rules
Taxes & Currency
  • CIT 20%; VAT 10% on sales
  • Local sales in VND; profit repatriation allowed post-tax
  • Plan for FX & supply chain variability

Note: EPR (recycling) rules are expanding; plan reverse-logistics for cylinders/machines.
